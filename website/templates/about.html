C.H.A.R.M (Computerised Hardware Automated Reception Manager) is a computerised robotic program that simulates and processes human conversation (either written or spoken), allowing us to interact with digital devices as if we are communicating with a real person. It is built with the objective of monitoring the reception area of our Institute with  certain features that aim to have conversation with individuals,  interact with them and display details of college as per their requirement. All Of Our team members have faced this kind of issues in our college that's why we thought of this. In simple words our robot is built in such a way that it will help students in providing the information related to the institute.


Well this project has helped us gain skills in domain of web development till now and also some kind of work experience of working as a team which helps us in improvment of leadership skills, team work and an environment of work which we assume is as similar as working for a company.The main motive is to get proficient in technologies and gain knowledge about hardware and software. Since we all belong to different branches of engineering, we have always willing to enhance our skills- set thus this project gave us opportunity to upgrade on the same and deepen our knowledge on various technologies. In addition to software , this project gave us exposure to know about hardware and increase our learnings in it.


CHARM has encourged us in learning software languages such as Python which will help us later in the inception of Artficial Intelligence and Machine Learning in the field of Robotics. Our Initiative to this project of CHARM will be of great help for everyone who want to get immediate response of the questions asked with a short description of the query. CHARM has been programmed in such a sophisticated way that it will be charming and attractive enough with his facial expressions and voice that everyone is willing to see in a robot. CHARM is a combination of automated processes and various technologies in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Robotics, Electronics,Computer Science and Web Development which makes it facinating with all the features that a robotic chatbot should have. This investment in a robotic chatbot would be appreciable as this could work in any domain which includes Q&A with satisfying replies. We all are so grateful to be in this project, that we got to explore various kind of technologies out of our branches and get to learn so many things which would definately gonna give an ease hand to students and society.
